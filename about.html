About Us

Learn more about our mission, vision, and core values.

Our Story

Mambilla Livestock and Agro Allieds Ltd is a forward-thinking agribusiness company committed to delivering high-quality livestock and agro-related products across Nigeria and beyond.

The company operates across the agricultural value chain, specializing in livestock breeding, rearing, processing, and distribution, while also engaging in agro-allied services that support sustainable food production and supply. With a strategic focus on innovation, efficiency, and sustainability, Mambilla Livestock aims to bridge the gap between traditional farming practices and modern agribusiness solutions, ensuring consistent value for customers, partners, and stakeholders.

At Mambilla Livestock and Agro Allieds Ltd, we are committed to enhancing food production systems while maintaining quality, safety, and sustainability. We strive to create value across the agricultural supply chain and contribute meaningfully to national development.

Our Mission

To sustainably produce and deliver high-quality livestock and agro-allied products through innovative practices, efficient systems, and a commitment to excellence, while contributing to food security and economic growth.

Our Vision

To become a leading and trusted agribusiness brand in Africa, recognized for excellence in livestock production, agro-allied services, and sustainable agricultural practices.
